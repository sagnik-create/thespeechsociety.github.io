The Speech Society

Welcome to The Speech Society (TSS) at UEM, Jaipur! Founded by passionate first-year students of the entry batch of 2022, our society stands as a beacon for fostering communication excellence.

Our tagline, "Think.Script.Speak," embodies our ethos. It encapsulates the essence of effective communication—first thinking about the message, then scripting or organizing thoughts, and finally expressing them verbally. At the heart of our mission is the profound importance of language. We advocate for the significance of language in all its forms and aspects, recognizing its pivotal role in personal and professional development.

Every student is warmly welcomed into our community with open arms. We encourage all those eager to embark on this enriching journey of self-improvement to join us. The Speech Society is a space where diversity thrives, and where every member's voice is valued.

Join us and discover the transformative power of effective communication. Let's embark on this journey of honing our communication skills and unlocking our potential.

University of Engineering and Management

UEM Jaipur, established in 2012, stands as a premier state private university. With an impressive highest placement offer of Rs 72 lakhs per annum and an average of Rs 6.5 lakhs, we prioritize academic excellence. Ranked 1st in North India by the Ministry of Education, Govt. of India, our commitment to innovation is evident through our recognition as a mentor institute by AICTE.

In just a decade, we've become a global leader, offering specialized programs in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and more. Our Physiotherapy department holds a 4-star rating from the Indian Association of Physiotherapists.

Throughout the pandemic, we maintained academic continuity, ensuring students graduated on time. Our campus spans 32 acres, featuring modern facilities, and our alumni are excelling in top companies worldwide, showcasing the success of UEM Jaipur’s education and placement initiatives.

Join UEM Jaipur for quality education, global recognition, and a pathway to a successful future!

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is The Speech Society?

    The Speech Society (TSS) is a vibrant community established by Alok Agarwal (Founder) and Somen Halder (Co-founder), dedicated to fostering and enhancing communication skills among individuals. TSS serves as a nurturing space where members are encouraged to develop and refine their abilities to articulate thoughts effectively, thereby nurturing their confidence and leadership potential.

    Recognizing the growing significance of language and communication in today's world, TSS emphasizes the pivotal role these skills play in personal, academic, and professional success. Through the vision of its founders and the dedication of its members, TSS continues to inspire and empower individuals to harness the power of language and communication for personal growth and success in an increasingly interconnected world.

  • Why was The Speech Society initiated?

    The Speech Society (TSS) was founded with a clear mission to empower individuals by enhancing their communication skills. Recognizing the pivotal role effective communication plays in personal, academic, and professional spheres, TSS emerged as a response to the growing need for a platform that fosters articulate expression, confidence, and leadership abilities. The founders, driven by a vision to bridge the gap between theoretical learning and practical communication proficiency, established TSS to create an inclusive space where students could refine their speaking, presentation, and interpersonal skills.

  • How did the idea of The Speech Society come to life?

    The inception of The Speech Society (TSS) was a collaborative effort nurtured by the dedication and guidance of our esteemed mentor, Prof Dr. Mukesh Yadav, who served as the faculty coordinator. With a shared vision to cultivate and elevate communication skills among students, TSS emerged through the enthusiastic initiative of its founding members, led by Alok Agarwal and Somen Halder. The collaborative efforts of the mentors, faculty, and passionate students laid the foundation for TSS, fostering an inclusive environment that values the transformative power of language and communication in shaping individuals' personal and professional growth.

  • Why should I join The Speech Society?

    Becoming a part of our community offers an invaluable opportunity to unlock your full communication potential in a supportive and dynamic community. TSS provides a platform where individuals can cultivate and refine their speaking, writing, and presentation skills in a nurturing environment. By joining TSS, you become part of a vibrant network that encourages personal growth, boosts confidence, and fosters leadership abilities. Moreover, in an increasingly interconnected world, where effective communication is paramount, TSS equips you with the tools and experiences necessary to navigate diverse linguistic and cultural landscapes. Joining TSS isn't just about honing communication—it's about embarking on a journey of personal growth, and empowerment, and becoming a more impactful and confident communicator in various facets of life.

  • What is the mission of The Speech Society?

    Our mission is to empower individuals by cultivating effective communication skills through diverse activities, events, and competitions. We strive to foster an inclusive community that values the importance of language, encourages personal growth, and inspires confidence in articulation, critical thinking, and leadership abilities. TSS endeavours to empower UEMJ students with the linguistic proficiency and confidence necessary to excel academically, professionally, and socially in a globalised world.

  • What is the vision of The Speech Society?

    The vision of the Speech Society (TSS) is to be a catalyst for transformation, empowering individuals within our community to communicate with confidence, clarity, and conviction. We aspire to cultivate an environment where effective communication is not just a skill but a cornerstone of personal growth and collective achievement. Our vision is to create a supportive platform where members flourish, inspired by the belief that communication is the key to unlocking one's full potential and shaping a better, more connected world.
